Summer Chocolate Mimosa Tree

Summer Chocolate Mimosa Tree. The tree has a wide-spreading crown with finely divided leaves and in summer the tree is covered with clusters of beautiful large pink and white flowers that look like tufts of silk threads the crest on the head of a peacock or a powder puff. It lends an extremely fine and delicate texture to the landscape composition which can make it a great accent feature on this basis alone.
