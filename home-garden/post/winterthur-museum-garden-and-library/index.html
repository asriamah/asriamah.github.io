Winterthur Museum Garden And Library

Winterthur Museum Garden And Library. The library at the Henry Francis du Pont Winterthur Museum is a research center that contains a wide variety of written and visual materials that promote the interdisciplinary study of American art material culture and everyday life in America into the 20th century. We invite you to visit us onsite or online.
